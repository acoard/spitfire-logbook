# NOTES
- Bottom of the page, as a prose paragraph (dated May 30 1944): On May 30 1944 I was posted from 84 G.S.U. (RAF Acton Down) to 313 Squadron in 2 Tactical Air Force. I'd had my pilot's wings for 30 months - and arrived on a Grey Spitfire Mk. IX at a temporary airfield at Appledean - S.A.T. Billet. We were there about a week before the Invasion. May 30 1944. S.G.T.
