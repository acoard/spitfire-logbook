# NOTES
- Bottom right, section starting with "On May 30/1944": On May 30/1944 I was posted from 84 G.S.U (R.A.F Acton Down) to 313 Squadron in 2 Tactical Air Force - I'd lived my a Good Spitfire pilot's wing for 30 months - and arrived on Convoy Patrol at Chudleigh, near Teignmouth. We were under canvas. It was just a week before the invasion.
